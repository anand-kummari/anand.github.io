About

I am an Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Engineer with 6+ years of experience building and scaling production-grade intelligent systems across FinTech, computer vision, and generative AI domains. I specialize in taking ideas from research concepts to reliable customer-facing products — designing architectures, building data pipelines, deploying models, and ensuring they operate at scale in real environments. My work spans large-language-model applications, retrieval-augmented systems, real-time inference pipelines, and cloud-native ML platforms on AWS and GCP.

Throughout my career I have led end-to-end initiatives including automated root-cause analysis platforms, large-scale language identification services, visual recommendation engines, and traffic intelligence systems. I regularly collaborate with product, engineering, and operations teams to translate business problems into measurable technical solutions, and I enjoy mentoring engineers while improving reliability and maintainability of ML systems. I focus on building practical AI that delivers impact — not just models that work in notebooks, but systems that businesses depend on every day.
